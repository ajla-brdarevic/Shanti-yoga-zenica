About Me

We store so much in our bodies and through movement, sweat and breath we are able to release it, renew it and rewire our neurons. I felt that I have found a spring of pure gold and felt strongly to share it. To spread techniques of Yoga is my life Dharma. I believe that Yoga is the way of healing the world one person at a time. As we feel better in our body, heart and mind, we are able to make new conscious choices instead of being stuck like a broken record, continuing to hurt ourselves and others. Now more than ever, we need to learn to love and care for ourselves, this Planet and all living beings. This is the most important work we can do in our lives. More...
