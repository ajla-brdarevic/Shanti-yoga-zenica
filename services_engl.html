Hatha and Yin Yoga

Transform your body through our dynamic Hatha Yoga sessions. In addition to strengthening muscles and making the body more resilient for everyday challenges, Hatha yoga teaches you mindfulness through the alignment of various body postures and awareness of breath. Here, physical strength merges with deeper inner understanding, preparing you for greater possibilities in life.
Relax and allow your body to open up to new dimensions through long, passive postures in our Yin Yoga sessions. Dive deeply into these poses, releasing tension and promoting flexibility and energy flow throughout the body, focusing on ligaments, joints, bones, and the deepest tissues of the body.

The Awakening Program

PThe Awakening Program lasts for 3 months and is specifically designed to introduce beginners to yoga as well as to deepen an already established yoga practice:
1st month – We introduce awareness into our physical body with exercises for stretching eye muscles, neck, shoulders, and hips. We familiarize ourselves with basic asanas (poses), learn to identify and reduce stress levels in our bodies. We work on aligning the mind and body, acquaint ourselves with our breath, and learn proper breathing techniques.
2nd month – We learn Sun Salutation and other asanas, new types of pranayama (breathing) for relaxation, stress management, and anxiety regulation.
3rd month – We acquaint ourselves with the energetic, subtle body and the 7 energy centers (chakras) and their individual purposes and impacts on our physical body. We practice special asanas designed to open, balance, and align chakras.

Join us on the Path of transforming yourself as well as the world around you.
